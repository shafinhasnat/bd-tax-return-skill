# IT-11GA / IT-10B / IT-10BB — Official Return Form Fields

**Source:** Official NBR IT-11GA (2023) form, Income Tax Act 2023

These are the legal/paper return fields. eReturn portal mirrors these (see `ereturn-portal-tabs.md` for the online flow).

---

## IT-11GA Main Return — Personal Block

1. Name of the Taxpayer
2. National ID No. / Passport No. (if no NID)
3. TIN (12-digit)
4. (a) Circle  (b) Taxes Zone
5. Assessment Year
6. Residential Status (Resident / Non-resident)
7. Taxpayer's Status (Individual / Firm / HUF / Others)
8. Special-benefit ticks: war-wounded freedom fighter / Female / Third Gender / Disabled person / Aged 65+ / Parent of disabled person
9. Date of Birth (DD MM YYYY)
10. Spouse name + TIN (if spouse is taxpayer)
11. Address + Telephone + Mobile + Email
12. Employer's name (latest, if multiple employments)
13. (a) Name of Organization  (b) BIN
14. Partners/Members name + TIN (if Firm/AoP)

## IT-10B (2023) — Statement of Assets, Liabilities and Expenses

**Mandatory if ANY of:**
- Total assets (home + abroad) > BDT 40,00,000
- Owns a Motor Car at any time
- Has invested in House Property/Apartment within City Corp area
- Owns assets outside Bangladesh
- Is a Shareholder Director of a Company
- All Public Servants (mandatory regardless)

### Section 1 — Sources of Fund
- (a) Total Income (from IT-11GA Sl. 11)
- (b) Tax-Exempted Income
- (c) Receipt of Gift and others
- **Total Sources of Fund**

### Section 2-7 — Wealth Reconciliation
- 2. Net Wealth as on Last Date of Previous Income Year
- 3. Sum of Source of Fund + Previous Year's Net Wealth (2+3) [note: KPMG/form labels this 1+2]
- 4. (a) Lifestyle Expense (per IT-10BB) + (b) Gift/Expenses/Loss not in IT-10BB
- 5. **Net Wealth at Last Date of this Financial Year (3 – 4)**
- 6. Personal Liabilities Outside Business: (a) Institutional / (b) Non-Institutional / (c) Other
- 7. **Gross Wealth (5 + 6)**

### Section 8 — Particulars of Assets (within Bangladesh)
- (a) Total Asset of Business – Business Liabilities = **Business Capital**
- (b) Director's Shareholdings in Companies
- (c) Business Capital of Partnership Firm
- (d) Non-Agricultural Property / Land / House Property
- (e) Agricultural Property
- (f) Financial Assets:
  - (i) Share / Debenture / Bond / Securities / Unit Certificate
  - (ii) Sanchaypatra / DPS
  - (iii) Loan given (with Name & NID of Receiver)
  - (iv) Savings Deposit / Term Deposit (FDR)
  - (v) Provident Fund or Other Fund
  - (vi) Other Investment
- (g) Motor Vehicle(s) — cost incl. registration; mention type & registration number
- (h) Ornaments (mention quantity)
- (i) Furniture and Electronic Items
- (j) Other Assets
- (k) Cash in Hand and Fund Outside Business: (i) Bank Balance / (ii) Cash / (iii) Others
- **Total assets within Bangladesh**

### Section 9 — Asset Outside Bangladesh
### Section 10 — Total Assets in BD + Outside BD (8 + 9)

**Coverage:** assets and liabilities of self, spouse (if not assessee), minor children and dependants must all be included.

## Verification (mandatory)

Solemn declaration + signature on each of: IT-11GA, IT-10B, IT-10BB. For natural persons, signatures on IT-10B and IT-10BB are also mandatory.

## Required Enclosures (Paper Return Only — NOT for eReturn)

> ⚠️ The list below applies to **paper** IT-11GA submission. The **eReturn portal at etaxnbr.gov.bd does NOT accept uploads** of any of these. For online filing, the taxpayer keeps these documents in their own files for audit/assessment purposes only.

For paper return, the following must be enclosed per the official Instructions:
- Salary statement (for salary)
- Bank statement (for interest)
- Certificate for interest on savings instruments
- Rent agreement, municipal tax receipts, land revenue receipts, house property loan interest statement, insurance premium for house property
- Statement of Professional income (per IT Rule-8)
- Assessment/income statement & balance sheet for partnership income
- Documents for capital gain
- Dividend warrant for dividend income
- Statement of other income
- Investment proofs: savings certificates, LIP, DPS, Zakat, stock/share
- Depreciation Chart (if claiming depreciation)
- Computation of Income per Income Tax Act, 2023

# Source Citations

**Compiled:** 2026-04-20

## Primary (Authoritative)

1. **Finance Ordinance 2025** — gazetted 22 June 2025. Sets rates for AY 2025-26 (effective) and AY 2026-27 / 2027-28 (newly proposed).
2. **Income Tax Act 2023** — replaced Income Tax Ordinance 1984.
3. **NBR IT-11GA (2023) form** — official return for natural persons. PDF source: nbr.gov.bd/uploads/form/Individual_Return_IT-11Ga(2023)5.pdf
4. **NBR website:** https://nbr.gov.bd/ ; eReturn portal: https://etaxnbr.gov.bd/

## Secondary (Cross-Verified)

5. **KPMG / Rahman Rahman Huq — Salient Features of Finance Ordinance 2025** (June 2025 PDF) — used as PRIMARY source for slabs, surcharge bands, minimum tax, rebate formula, employment income rules.
6. **PwC Worldwide Tax Summaries — Bangladesh Individual** (taxsummaries.pwc.com) — cross-checked slabs, thresholds, rebate worked example.
7. **ICAB Finance Ordinance 2025 / Income Tax Act 2023 summary (3 Sep 2025)** by Sarker Nahidul Islam FCA.

## Reconciled Conflicts

- **30% top tax rate** for AY 2025-26: KPMG and PwC confirm; ACE Advisory and LegalSeba omit it. Treated as confirmed.
- **Rebate cap**: PwC's worked example, KPMG, and ICAB all confirm BDT 10,00,000 (not 15 lakh as some secondary sources state).
- **Salary deduction ceiling**: BDT 4,50,000 for AY 2025-26 → BDT 5,00,000 for AY 2026-27 (Finance Ord 2025). Confirmed by Taxpert, port-zone, bdtaxcalac.

## Update Cadence

- Re-verify minimum tax + threshold figures from NBR's Income Tax Paripatra (annual circular).

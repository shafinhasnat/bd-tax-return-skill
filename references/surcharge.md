# Surcharge — Net Wealth & Environmental

**Source:** Finance Ordinance 2025, KPMG Salient Features

---

## 1. Net Wealth Surcharge (Individuals)

Applies on **total income tax payable** (not on income).

| Total net wealth (BDT) | Trigger | AY 2025-26 | AY 2026-27 | AY 2027-28 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ≤ 4 crore (4,00,00,000) AND ≤1 motor car AND no >8,000 sft house | – | 0% | 0% | 0% |
| > 4 crore – 10 crore, OR owner of >1 motor car, OR owner of house >8,000 sft | Any one trigger | **10%** | 10% | 10% |
| > 10 crore – 20 crore | Net wealth | **20%** | 20% | 20% |
| > 20 crore – 50 crore | Net wealth | **30%** | 30% | 30% |
| > 50 crore | Net wealth | **35%** | 35% | 35% |

Bands UNCHANGED across all three years.

**Trigger note (lowest band):** A taxpayer with net wealth ≤ 4 crore still pays 10% surcharge if they own >1 motor car or own a house property >8,000 sft.

**Computation:**
```
net_wealth_surcharge = applicable_rate × tax_payable_before_surcharge
```

## 2. Environmental Surcharge (Motor Cars)

Levied per fiscal year on owners of **more than one** motor car. Surcharge is exempted on the car with the LOWEST applicable rate (i.e., applied on additional cars).

| Engine capacity | Surcharge per car (BDT) |
|---|---|
| Up to 1500cc / 75kW | 25,000 |
| 1500cc–2000cc (75–100 kW) | 50,000 |
| 2000cc–2500cc (100–125 kW) | 75,000 |
| 2500cc–3000cc (125–150 kW) | 1,50,000 |
| 3000cc–3500cc (150–175 kW) | 2,00,000 |
| Above 3500cc / 175kW | 3,50,000 |

**KEY CHANGE (Finance Ord 2025):** Environmental surcharge for **electric motor cars** is **withdrawn from AY 2026-27 onwards**.

Collection mechanism: levied during vehicle registration or fitness renewal (BRTA collects).

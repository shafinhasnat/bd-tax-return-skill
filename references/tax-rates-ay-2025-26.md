# AY 2025-26 — Tax Rates for Individual Taxpayers

**Income year:** 1 July 2024 – 30 June 2025
**Return due:** 30 November 2025 (extensions possible via Commissioner of Taxes, up to 90 days)
**Source of authority:** Finance Act 2024, Income Tax Act 2023
**Verified against:** KPMG Rahman Rahman Huq Salient Features of Finance Ordinance 2025; PwC Bangladesh Tax Summary; LegalSeba 2025-26 Guide

---

## Tax Slabs (Individual & Firm)

| Total annual income (BDT) | Rate |
|---|---|
| First 3,50,000 | Nil |
| Next 1,00,000 | 5% |
| Next 4,00,000 | 10% |
| Next 5,00,000 | 15% |
| Next 5,00,000 | 20% |
| Next 20,00,000 | 25% |
| Balance | 30% |

**Note:** The 30% top rate was introduced via Finance Act 2024 and applies first in AY 2024-25.

## Tax-Free Threshold (Initial Exemption)

| Category | Threshold (BDT) |
|---|---|
| General taxpayer | 3,50,000 |
| Women & senior citizens (age 65+) | 4,00,000 |
| Third gender | 4,75,000 |
| Physically challenged persons | 4,75,000 |
| Gazetted war-wounded freedom fighters & "July Warriors" | 5,00,000 |
| Parent/legal guardian of a physically challenged person | +50,000 above the applicable category (only one parent if both are taxpayers) |

## Salary Income Exemption (Schedule 6, Part 1, Para 27)

For employees other than government pay-scale employees:
- **Exempted amount = LOWER of:**
  - 1/3 of total salary income, OR
  - **BDT 4,50,000**

Government employees follow separate Schedule 1(a) treatment (specific allowances individually exempt).

## Non-residents (other than Bangladeshi non-residents)

Flat 30% on total income.

---

## Caveats

- AY 2025-26 is the **last year** that location-based minimum tax applies (see `minimum-tax.md`).
- Tax rates were **unchanged from AY 2024-25** to AY 2025-26.
- LegalSeba's 2025-26 guide omits the 30% top slab — likely outdated; KPMG and PwC confirm 30% applies.

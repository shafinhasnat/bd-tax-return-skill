# AY 2026-27 — Tax Rates for Individual Taxpayers

**Income year:** 1 July 2025 – 30 June 2026
**Return due:** 30 November 2026 (extensions possible via Commissioner of Taxes, up to 90 days)
**Source of authority:** Finance Ordinance 2025 (gazetted 22 June 2025), Income Tax Act 2023
**Verified against:** KPMG Rahman Rahman Huq Salient Features of Finance Ordinance 2025; PwC Bangladesh worked example; multiple Bangladeshi practitioner guides

**Note:** Same rates apply for AY 2026-27 AND AY 2027-28.

---

## Tax Slabs (Individual, excluding Firm)

| Total annual income (BDT) | Rate |
|---|---|
| First 3,75,000 | Nil |
| Next 3,00,000 | 10% |
| Next 4,00,000 | 15% |
| Next 5,00,000 | 20% |
| Next 20,00,000 | 25% |
| Balance | 30% |

**KEY CHANGE vs AY 2025-26:** The 5% slab is **removed**. First taxable slab starts at 10%.

## Tax-Free Threshold (Initial Exemption)

| Category | Threshold (BDT) |
|---|---|
| General taxpayer | 3,75,000 |
| Women & senior citizens (age 65+) | 4,25,000 |
| Third gender | 5,00,000 |
| Physically challenged persons | 5,00,000 |
| Gazetted war-wounded freedom fighters & "July Warriors" | 5,25,000 |
| Parent/legal guardian of a physically challenged person | +50,000 above the applicable category (only one parent if both are taxpayers) |

All thresholds raised by BDT 25,000 vs AY 2025-26.

## Salary Income Exemption (Schedule 6, Part 1)

For employees other than government pay-scale employees (per Finance Ord 2025):
- **Exempted amount = LOWER of:**
  - 1/3 of total salary income, OR
  - **BDT 5,00,000** (raised from BDT 4,50,000)

## Non-residents (other than Bangladeshi non-residents)

Flat 30% on total income.

---

## Summary of Key Changes from AY 2025-26

| Item | AY 2025-26 | AY 2026-27 |
|---|---|---|
| Tax-free threshold (general) | 3,50,000 | 3,75,000 |
| First taxable slab | 5% on next 1,00,000 | 10% on next 3,00,000 |
| Salary exemption ceiling | 4,50,000 | 5,00,000 |
| Minimum tax | Area-based (5,000/4,000/3,000) | Flat 5,000 (1,000 for new taxpayers) |
| Surcharge bands | Unchanged | Unchanged |
| Rebate formula | Unchanged | Unchanged |
